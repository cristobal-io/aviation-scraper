Adria Airways destinations

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

As of 2015, Adria Airways operates to 22 scheduled destinations from Ljubljana 4 from Pristina, 4 from Tirana, 2 from Lodz, 1 from Maribor.[1] The company also operates to 2 regular charter destinations from Ljubljana (18 in summer), 2 from Maribor and 3 from Pristina.[2][3]
